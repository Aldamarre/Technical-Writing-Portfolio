# Aldamarre Jean – Technical Writing Portfolio  
CIS311 – Technical Writing in CIS  
Post University

This portfolio contains all major writing assignments I completed throughout my Technical Writing course, arranged in numerical order based on the unit they belong to. Each assignment highlights different writing skills, including workplace communication, summaries, research collection, technical proposal writing, and a full research report. The goal of this portfolio is to show my growth as a writer and my ability to communicate clearly in professional and technical environments.

---

## Unit 1.1 – The Power of Workplace Messaging  
**File:** `Unit_1.1_Workplace_Messaging.docx`

This essay explains how workplace tools like Monday.com, Slack, and Confluence help remote and hybrid teams stay organized and connected. I discuss how each platform supports communication, project management, and teamwork. This assignment shows my understanding of how businesses use digital tools to stay efficient and maintain strong communication.

## Social Media and Online Content Analysis  
**File:** `Social_Media_and_Content_Analysis.docx`

This shows my ability to analyze online communication and understand how companies shape their digital identity.

## Unit 3.2 – Summaries and Abstracts  
**File:** `Unit_3.2_Summaries_and_Abstracts.docx`

This assignment shows my skill in condensing complex information into clear summaries.

## Unit 6.1 – Technical Proposal Writing  
**File:** `Unit_6.1_Technical_Proposal.docx`

This full technical proposal recommends replacing campus wired exit signs with self-illuminating tritium-based signs. It includes an executive summary, problem statement, cost breakdown, supporting evidence, and a detailed implementation plan. It demonstrates my ability to write structured, professional proposals similar to what is used in real workplace settings.

## Cybersecurity and Data Breaches: Ethical Responsibilities and Consumer Protection  
**File:** `Cybersecurity_and_Data_Breaches.docx`

This assignment reflects my ability to combine academic research with practical explanations in a clear and organized way.
